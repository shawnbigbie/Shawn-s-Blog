About Me

Don't count the days, make the days count

Born in South Jordan, Utah, I found a love for technology at a young age. I became curious as to how everything I was using functioned. Many years later, I found myself creating Website and Designing including Logos, Marketing, and Programming. After doing this for my personal enjoyment for many years, I finally feel like I developed the skill set to go out and contribute my skills to projects and help people create their idea to come true. As of now, I take advantage of all the skills I have learned and still am learning at Utah Valley University and can provide multiple services, ranging from mock up design to general tutoring to straight up programming.

I'v been working in the web development and design career for 5+ years, this incluids working for small startup companys and for the last year been working from home as a freelancer and starting up my own small company.

My favorite part of web development is that there are so many options from career paths from languages to learn for students and people to explore and fine what thier good at and works with them.

What I expect out of this class is not to just learn more about ES6 JavaScript but also learn a little bit about each JS frameworks like Vue, React, and Angular so students and myself can focus on one path and start learning skills that jobs are looking for.
